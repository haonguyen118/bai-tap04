A website (also written as a website) is one or more web pages and related content that is identified by a common domain name and publishes on at least one web serve. Websites are typically dedicated to paticular topic or purpose, such as new, education, commerce, entertainment, or social media.Hyperlinking between web pages guides the navigation of the site, which often starts with a home page. The most-visited sites are Google, Youtube, and Facebook. All publicly-accessible websites collectively constilute the World wide web. There are also private websites that can onlys be accessed on a private network, such as a company's internal website for its employeer. Users can access websites on a range of devices, including desktops, laptops,tablets, and smartphones. The apps used on these devices is called a web browser.
